Job Title: Senior AI Engineer

Job Description
We are looking for a skilled AI Engineer to join our team. The ideal candidate should have strong experience in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning.

Responsibilities
- Design and implement ML models for text analysis.
- Work with large datasets using Pandas and NumPy.
- Deploy models using Docker and AWS.

Requirements
- Proficiency in Python and SQL.
- Strong knowledge of Scikit-Learn, NLTK, and spaCy.
- Experience with Deep Learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure).
- Good understanding of Git and version control.
- Experience with Streamlit is a plus.
